macOS Focus Utility

Block visual noise.
Keep one window in your lane.

AntiADHD is a menu bar Focus Mask for macOS. It dims the rest of your desktop so your attention stays on the window you are using right now.

What AntiADHD is

A lightweight accessory app that lives in the menu bar and toggles a focus mask with global hotkeys.

  • Built for macOS 13+
  • Works as an accessory app, no dock clutter
  • Falls back safely when permissions are missing

Modes

Choose the way you want to focus

Current Display

Keep only the monitor under your mouse fully visible while other displays stay masked.

Focused Window

Highlight the active window only. For non-selected regions you can use Freeze, Black Overlay, or Blur.

Locked Window

Pin visibility to one chosen window and keep it visible while you move your cursor around.

Hotkeys

Quick control without leaving flow

⌥⌘B

Toggle focus mask on and off.

⌥⌘L

Lock the currently focused window.

⌥⌘⎋

Emergency off to instantly clear overlays.

Permissions

Accessibility is required for window modes

Focused Window and Locked Window need macOS Accessibility permission. If permission is not granted, AntiADHD automatically falls back to Current Display mode.
