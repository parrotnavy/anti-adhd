What AntiADHD is

A lightweight accessory app that lives in the menu bar and toggles a focus mask with global hotkeys.

  • Works as an accessory app, no dock clutter

Modes

Choose the way you want to focus

Current Display

Move your mouse to a display and only that screen stays clear while the others stay masked.

Freeze Darken Blur

Focused Window

Keep the active window clear while the rest of the screen can freeze, darken, or blur.

Locked Window

Lock one window in place so it stays visible even while your cursor moves to other windows.

Hotkeys

Quick control without leaving flow

⌥⌘B

Toggle focus mask on and off.

⌥⌘L

Lock the currently focused window.

⌥⌘⎋

Emergency off to instantly clear overlays.

Permissions

Accessibility is required for window modes

Focused Window and Locked Window need macOS Accessibility permission. If permission is not granted, AntiADHD automatically falls back to Current Display mode.

What is AntiADHD?

AntiADHD is a macOS menu bar focus mask that reduces visual distraction by masking parts of the desktop outside the window or display you want to keep clear.

Who is it for?

It is built for Mac users who want a calmer workspace during writing, coding, reading, design, and other tasks that benefit from less screen noise.

Which macOS versions are supported?

AntiADHD is designed for macOS 13 and later.

Which permissions are required?

Focused Window and Locked Window need Accessibility permission. If that permission is not granted, the app safely falls back to Current Display mode.
